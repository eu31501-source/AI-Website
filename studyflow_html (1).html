What subjects does StudyFlow support? ▾
StudyFlow supports all major high school and university subjects including Mathematics, English, Chemistry, Physics, Biology, Accounting, History, Geography, Economics, and more. The AI adapts its explanations from Year 7 through university level.
Will the AI just give me the answers? ▾
No — StudyFlow is designed to help you learn, not do your work for you. It provides step-by-step explanations, hints, and guided reasoning so you develop a genuine understanding of each topic.
What file types can I upload? ▾
You can upload PDFs, Word documents (.docx), PowerPoint files (.pptx), images (JPG, PNG, HEIC), and photographs of handwritten work. The AI reads and analyzes all of these formats.

Absolutely. Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We never sell your data or use it to train models without explicit consent. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

Yes, you can cancel anytime with no fees or penalties. Your premium access continues until the end of your billing period, after which you'll be moved to the free plan automatically.

Can you explain how to solve quadratic equations by factoring? I'm in Year 11.
AI
Great question! Let's work through factoring quadratic equations step by step. 🎯

A quadratic equation has the form: ax² + bx + c = 0

Step 1: Identify a, b, and c
For x² + 5x + 6 = 0 → a=1, b=5, c=6
Step 2: Find two numbers that multiply to c (6) and add to b (5)
→ 2 × 3 = 6 ✓ and 2 + 3 = 5 ✓
Step 3: Write as factored form
(x + 2)(x + 3) = 0
Step 4: Apply zero product property
x + 2 = 0 → x = −2
x + 3 = 0 → x = −3
